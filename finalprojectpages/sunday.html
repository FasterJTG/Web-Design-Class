SUNDAY - IN HOCKEY

Oilers even series vs LA in Game 4 OT comeback.
April 27, 2025

After trailing 2-0 in the series vs. the Los Angeles Kings, the Edmonton Oilers have evened things up with a big OT win. By the midpoint of the second period, LA led 2-1, only extended to 3-1 when Kevin Fiala scored on a breakaway. However, early in the third, Edmonton's Evan Bruchard scored off of a deflection to cut the deficit to one. With only thirty seconds remaining and the net empty for Edmonton, Bruchard did it again, scoring on a slap shot to send Game 4 to overtime. With a minute remaining in the first OT period, a scramble for the puck ended in a deflection to Leon Draisitl, scoring to give the Oilers a 4-3 win and tying the first round best-of-seven series 2-2.
