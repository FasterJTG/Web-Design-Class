TUESDAY - PICKING UP THE PACE

Indiana advances with OT comeback vs. Milwaukee in Game 5.
April 29, 2025

In front of their home crowd in Indianapolis, the Pacers came back from a 118-111 deficit with 40 seconds remaining in OT to clinch the series 4-1 in Game 5. In a hotly contested matchup, the Milwaukee Bucks were up 13-0 and continued building on their lead until Indiana began to tie things up from there. The game would go back-and-forth the rest of the way. It was Tyrese Hailburton who sent the game into overtime with a dunk to tie the game at 103 with 10 seconds remaining in Q4. Eventually, in the OT session, Milwaukee led 118-111 with 40 seconds remaining, all but set to extend the series to Game 6. However, a pair of Bucks turnovers in the final seconds gave Indy life, and Hailburton was the hero again, scoring on a layup with 1 second remaining to win the game and the series 119-118.
