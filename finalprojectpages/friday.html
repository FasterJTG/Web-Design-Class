THE MIRACLE IN MANITOBA

Jets pull off miraculous comeback vs St. Louis in Game 7.
May 4, 2025

Down 2-0 at the end of the first period, 3-1 with two minutes remaining, the 1-seeded Winnipeg Jets have pulled off one of the greatest hockey playoff comebacks in recent memory. With time running out, Winnipeg emptied the net to start a 6 on 5 situation, something the 8-seeded St. Louis Blues have struggled with all season. Vladislav Namestnikov cut the deficit to one with a bounced puck that got past Blues netminder Jordan Binnington. After St. Louis' Pavel Buchnevich empty net shot in the final minute went high, and with time running out, Kyle Connor's desperation shot for Winnipeg went wide, but right into the stick of Cole Perfetti who scored on the open net with 1.6 seconds remaining. After overtime served as a stalemate, the game went into double overtime, where against the gassed Blues defense, Adam Lowry's game winning tip-in was all but a formality to complete the 4-3 comeback win and send Winnipeg to Dallas for the next round.
